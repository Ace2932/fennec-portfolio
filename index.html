🦊 Fennec — Autonomous Quadruped

Phase 1 · hardware bring-up

A four-legged robot taken from bare platform to walking autonomy — mechanical, electrical, and software all mine. Built on the open Nova-SM3 platform, redesigned into my own machine.

▸ Fennec learning to climb stairs — reinforcement-learning locomotion policy (MuJoCo simulation).
  • Autonomy: ROS 2 (Humble) on a Jetson Orin Nano Super — locomotion, calibration & safety nodes; Unitree LiDAR + RealSense for perception. Building toward VLA-model control, 3D SLAM, and mobile manipulation.
  • Custom power PCB: designed in KiCad — multi-rail battery distribution, INA226 per-rail telemetry, half-duplex servo-bus driver, E-stop chain, DRC/ERC clean.
  • Firmware: Teensy 4.1 real-time bus master (PlatformIO) driving 12× Feetech STS3215 serial-bus servos — joint mapping, hard-stop limits, watchdog liveness.
  • Safety-first + tested: pytest suites for preflight, safety-envelope, battery-shutdown, and hard-stop — robotics code that fails safe.
